# 📊 Amazon Prime Video Data Analysis (Power BI)

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes the Amazon Prime Video dataset using Power BI to uncover insights about content distribution, genres, ratings, and trends over time. The dashboard provides an interactive view of how content has evolved across different countries and categories.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze total titles available on Amazon Prime Video
* Understand content distribution by genre and rating
* Identify trends in content release over the years
* Compare Movies vs TV Shows
* Visualize country-wise content availability

---

## 📂 Dataset

* Source: Amazon Prime Titles Dataset
* Contains information such as:

  * Title
  * Genre
  * Director
  * Country
  * Release Year
  * Rating
  * Type (Movie / TV Show)

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

* KPI Cards:

  * Total Titles
  * Total Ratings
  * Total Genres
  * Total Directors
  * Start Year & End Year

* Visualizations:

  * Ratings Distribution
  * Genre-wise Content Count
  * Country-wise Content Map
  * Movies vs TV Shows (Donut Chart)
  * Content Growth Over Years (Line Chart)

---

## 🛠 Tools Used

* Power BI
* Power Query (Data Cleaning)
* DAX (Basic Calculations)

---

##  Key Insights

* Majority of content consists of Movies compared to TV Shows
* Drama and Comedy are the most dominant genres
* Significant growth in content after the year 2000
* Content is heavily concentrated in a few countries
